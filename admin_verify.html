Verification Protocols

Welcome to the Payment Verification Hub. This page is used to check the payments made by our users. When a user pays for a plan, they send us a UTR or Transaction ID. You must check this ID against our bank records to make sure the payment is real. If the payment is good, you can approve the request. This will automatically give the user the Ultimate Plan. They will be very happy to start playing their new games.

If you see a duplicate ID, the system will show you a red alert. This means someone might be trying to use the same payment twice. In this case, you should check very carefully or reject the request. We want to keep the arena fair for everyone who pays. Once you approve a request, the user will get a message on Telegram. They will then need to log in to the website again to see their new status. Thank you for your hard work in keeping the store honest and safe.

Make sure to process these requests fast. Most users expect their access within 10 to 30 minutes. If you are busy, please let the other admins know. We work together to provide the best support. Every approved payment helps our community grow and lets us add even more games to the library. Keep up the good work and stay focused on security.
