Deployment Guide

1

Place game files

Move your downloaded game data folder to your local Steam common folder:

...\Steam\steamapps\common\[Game Folder]\
2

Deploy Manifest

Download and drop the generated appmanifest.acf file into your main Steam apps directory:

...\Steam\steamapps\
3

Add Lua Patch

Download and drop the generated .lua file into your SteamTools script directory:

...\SteamTools\lua\
4

Boot & Play!

Ensure Steam is closed, start your SteamTools local patcher, start Steam, and click the green PLAY button!
