Admin Usage Instructions

        
This is the main hub for managing the Gamers Arena store. As an admin, you have the power to add new games to the library. When you add a new game, make sure to include all the account details. This helps our users get access to the games they love without any trouble. You can also manage premium bundles. These are the special games that users pay for to get personal accounts. Please check the prices carefully before you save any changes.

        
The Site Designer tool lets you edit text on the main pages. You can change titles, descriptions, and other text to keep the site looking fresh. Always remember to save your work before you leave a page. If you make a mistake, you can always come back here and fix it. Our goal is to provide the best service to our gaming community. Your work here makes that possible. Thank you for keeping the arena running smoothly.

        
If you have any questions about how to use these tools, please check the HQ manual. We want to make sure that all game drops are high quality and secure. Security is our number one priority. Never share your admin login with anyone else. Keep the database safe and help our members enjoy their gaming time. We are a team, and your help is very important to us.
