Secure Data Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

At Gamers Arena, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and privacy for our global community. This policy outlines how we handle your information within our secure ecosystem.

1. Data Collection Protocols

We collect minimal data required to maintain account security and provide access to the Arena assets:

  • Account Identifiers: Your email address and encrypted password for authentication.
  • Transactional Metadata: UTR/Transaction IDs submitted for membership verification. We do not store sensitive bank or card details.
  • Usage Logs: Records of your daily reveal count to manage plan limits.

2. Utilization of Information

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Access Management: Verifying your membership tier and unlocking library credentials.
  • Community Support: Providing personalized technical assistance via our Telegram Hub.
  • System Integrity: Monitoring for automated bot activity or unauthorized account modifications.

Gamers Arena does NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with external marketing entities.

3. Encryption & Security

We implement industry-standard AES-256 encryption for all data at rest. Communication between your browser and our servers is secured via 128-bit SSL/TLS encryption. Access to our backend databases is restricted to authorized HQ administrators only.

4. Third-Party Analytics

We utilize tools like Google Analytics to understand site performance. These services may collect non-identifiable metadata such as browser version, device type, and regional location to help us optimize the platform's speed and accessibility.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your profile data for the duration of your active membership. If you wish to purge your data and close your account, you may submit a "Data Deletion Request" via our official Telegram support channel. All associated metadata will be permanently erased from our primary servers within 72 hours.
