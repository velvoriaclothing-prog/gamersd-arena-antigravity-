Why Create an Account?

Library Access

Having an account at Gamers Arena lets you track your game reveals. You can see a list of the drops you have used in the past. This is very helpful for busy gamers who want to find their login details quickly. You won't have to search through thousands of games to find the one you played yesterday.

Elite Membership

Members can upgrade to our Ultimate Plan. This plan gives you unlimited access to our big database of 3,966+ games. If you don't have an account, you can only see public drops. Membership also gives you 24/7 support on Telegram. Our team is always ready to help you if you have any trouble with your games.

Safe and Fast

We care about your safety. Our system works with our Telegram bot to verify your payments fast. We use simple transaction IDs to activate your membership in minutes. When you join, you become part of a community that loves high-quality games. We make sure everything is easy to use and safe for everyone.

How to Use Our Service

1. Use Offline Mode: It is very important to use Offline Mode. After you log in to Steam or Epic, please switch to Offline Mode right away. This helps keep the accounts working for everyone. Playing online is not allowed.

2. Keep Accounts Safe: Please do not change any passwords. Do not try to add your own email or phone number to the accounts. Our system can see if you try to change things. If you do, your account might be banned forever.

3. Fair Use: Our Ultimate Plan lets you see many games. But please use it fairly. Do not use bots to scrape our data. Your account is just for you. If you share your login with other people, we might have to close your account.

4. Getting Help: If you need help, please reach out to us on Telegram. We have a great support group that can answer your questions. We want you to have the best gaming experience possible.

5. Daily Updates: We add new games every single day. Make sure to check the library often so you don't miss any new drops. We listen to our members and add the games they want to play the most.
