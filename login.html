Why Create an Account?

Library Persistence

Creating an account at Gamers Arena allows you to track your game reveals and maintain a history of the drops you've accessed. This persistence is crucial for frequent gamers who need to revisit credentials or community notes for specific AAA titles without searching through thousands of listings every time.

Elite Membership Status

Authenticated users can upgrade to our Ultimate Plan, which provides unlimited reveals for our entire database of 3,966+ games. Without an account, you are limited to public drops only. Membership unlocks a 24/7 VIP support channel on Telegram, ensuring that any technical hurdles in your gaming session are resolved within minutes by our expert administrators.

Secure & Automated

We prioritize your digital security. Our authentication system integrates seamlessly with our Telegram verification bot, using advanced transaction ID matching to activate memberships instantly. By registering, you join a community of thousands of elite gamers who value high-performance shared access and secure digital asset management.

Security & Usage Guidelines

1. Mandatory Offline Mode: To protect the integrity of shared accounts, all users MUST switch to Offline Mode in the Steam, Epic, or Ubisoft client immediately after logging in. Online play is strictly prohibited and monitored.

2. No Credential Alteration: Attempting to change passwords, enable two-factor authentication (Steam Guard), or modify account emails is a violation of our terms. Our system automatically detects these attempts and results in a permanent hardware ID ban from the Gamers Arena network.

3. Fair Usage Policy: While our Ultimate Plan offers unlimited reveals, we maintain a fair usage monitoring system to prevent botting and account scraping. Your account is for personal use; sharing your Gamers Arena credentials with third parties will lead to immediate suspension.
